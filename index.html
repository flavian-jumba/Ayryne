About Me

5+ Year's Experience as a Digital marketer

My name is Irene Njeri, and I have been a digital marketer for five years. During my time in university, I developed a keen interest in digital marketing and worked on several gigs, particularly in SEO. Over time, this interest grew into a full-fledged passion for the field.

I love digital marketing for many reasons, but what excites me the most is its ability to serve as a gateway to the infinite marketplace. I believe that maximizing digital marketing directly influences sales growth and empowers businesses to thrive in a competitive world.

My core competencies include Social Media Marketing, Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Content Writing, Branding, Email Marketing, and creating effective ad campaigns.

I am proficient in tools like Meta Business Suite, Google Ads (including YouTube Ads), Mailchimp, MailerLite, Google Analytics, and Google Search Console.

What sets me apart as a digital marketer is my tailored, client-centric approach, a proven track record of success, and the ability to simplify complex strategies for my clients. I have helped businesses grow by boosting social media engagement, creating effective ad campaigns, optimizing websites for search engines, implementing email marketing strategies, and executing bulk SMS campaigns. When I’m not creating content or running campaigns, you’ll find me exploring new tech trends or enjoying books by African authors.

If you’re ready to elevate your digital presence, let’s work together to achieve your goals.
